Executive Summary

Company Overview

boAt is India's leading consumer electronics brand specializing in audio products, wearables, and mobile accessories. Founded in 2016, the company has emerged as a dominant force in the affordable premium segment.

Market Position

Currently holds 32% market share in India's audio market and ranks as the world's 5th largest wearable brand, with strong presence in earbuds and wireless speakers.
