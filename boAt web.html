Executive Summary

Company Overview

boAt is India's leading consumer electronics brand specializing in audio products, wearables, and mobile accessories. Founded in 2016, the company has emerged as a dominant force in the affordable premium segment.

Market Position

Currently holds 32% market share in India's audio market and ranks as the world's 5th largest wearable brand, with strong presence in earbuds and wireless speakers.

Company Background
Foundation & Evolution
2016: Founded by Aman Gupta and Sameer Mehta as Imagine Marketing
2017-2018: Initial focus on mobile accessories and charging cables
2019: Pivoted to audio products, launched first wireless earbuds
2020-2021: Rapid expansion in TWS (True Wireless Stereo) market
2022-2024: Market leadership in India, global expansion plans
Brand Philosophy

"Plug into Nirvana" - boAt positions itself as a lifestyle brand that merges fashion with functionality, targeting young, tech-savvy consumers who value style and affordability.
